The Gray Image Of Constacyclic Codes Over The Finite Chain Ring \(f_{p^m}[u]/\langle U^k\rangle\)

Yuan Cao, Yonglin Cao . Journal of Applied Mathematics and Computing 2017 â€“ 2 citations

[Paper]    

Let \(\mathbb{F}{p^m}\) be a finite field of cardinality \(p^m\), where \(p\) is a prime, and \(k, N\) be any positive integers. We denote \(R_k=F{p^m}[u]/\langle u^k\rangle =F_{p^m}+uF_{p^m}+\ldots+u^{k-1}F_{p^m}\) (\(u^k=0\)) and \(\lambda=a_0+a_1u+\ldots+a_{k-1}u^{k-1}\) where \(a_0, a_1,\ldots, a_{k-1}\in F_{p^m}\) satisfying \(a_0\neq 0\) and \(a_1=1\). Let \(r\) be a positive integer satisfying \(p^{r-1}+1\leq k\leq p^r\). We defined a Gray map from \(R_k\) to \(F_{p^m}^{p^r}\) first, then prove that the Gray image of any linear \(\lambda\)-constacyclic code over \(R_k\) of length \(N\) is a distance invariant linear \(a_0^{p^r}\)-constacyclic code over \(F_{p^m}\) of length \(p^rN\). Furthermore, the generator polynomials for each linear \(\lambda\)-constacyclic code over \(R_k\) of length \(N\) and its Gray image are given respectively. Finally, some optimal constacyclic codes over \(F_{3}\) and \(F_{5}\) are constructed.
