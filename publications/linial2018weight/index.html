On The Weight Distribution Of Random Binary Linear Codes

Nati Linial, Jonathan Mosheiff . Random Structures & Algorithms 2019 â€“ 14 citations

[Paper]    
Rss

We investigate the weight distribution of random binary linear codes. For \(0<\lambda<1\) and \(n\to\infty\) pick uniformly at random \(\lambda n\) vectors in \(\mathbb{F}_2^n\) and let \(C \le \mathbb{F}_2^n\) be the orthogonal complement of their span. Given \(0<\gamma<1/2\) with \(0< \lambda < h(\gamma)\) let \(X\) be the random variable that counts the number of words in \(C\) of Hamming weight \(\gamma n\). In this paper we determine the asymptotics of the moments of \(X\) of all orders \(o(\frac{n}{log n})\).
