We take the spaces introduced by Laakso in 2000 and building on the work of
Barlow, Bass, Kumagai, and Teplyaev prove the existence and uniqueness of a
local symmetry invariant diffusion via heat kernel estimates. This work also
builds upon works of Cheeger, Barlow and Bass, as well as the author.
