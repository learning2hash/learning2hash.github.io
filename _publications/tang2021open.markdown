Let \(n=2m\). In the present paper, we study the binomial Boolean functions of
the form $\(f_\{a,b\}(x) = \mathrm\{Tr\}_1^\{n\}(a x^\{2^m-1 \})
+\mathrm\{Tr\}_1^\{2\}(bx^\{\frac\{2^n-1\}\{3\} \}), \)\( where \)m\( is an even positive
integer, \)a\in \mathbb\{F\}_\{2^n\}^*\( and \)b\in \mathbb\{F\}_4^*\(. We show that \)
f_\{a,b\}\( is a bent function if the Kloosterman sum
\)\(K_\{m\}\left(a^\{2^m+1\}\right)=1+ \sum_\{x\in \mathbb\{F\}_\{2^m\}^*\}
(-1)^\{\mathrm\{Tr\}_1^\{m\}(a^\{2^m+1\} x+ \frac\{1\}\{x\})\}\)\( equals \)4$, thus settling
an open problem of Mesnager. The proof employs tools including computing Walsh
coefficients of Boolean functions via multiplicative characters, divisibility
properties of Gauss sums, and graph theory.
