We consider 2-step free-Carnot groups equipped with sub-Finsler distances. We
prove that the metric spheres are codimension-one rectifiable from the
Euclidean viewpoint. The result is obtained by studying how the Lipschitz
constant for the distance function behaves near abnormal geodesics.
