We provide simple examples of two-color exchangeable sequences \(\xi=(\xi_1,
\xi_2, \ldots, \xi_n)\) that are not exchangeable. This answers a question of
Bladt and Shaiderman~\cite[Question 2.6]\{bladt2019characterisation\} for finite
two-color exchangeable sequences.
