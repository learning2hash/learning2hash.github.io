We provide a permutation invariant version of the strong law of large numbers
for exchangeable sequences of random variables. The proof consists of a
combination of the Koml\'\{o\}s-Berkes theorem, the usual strong law of large
numbers for exchangeable sequences and de Finetti's theorem.
