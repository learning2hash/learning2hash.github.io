Let \\(A_2(n,d)\\) be the maximum size of a binary code of length \\(n\\) and minimum
distance \\(d\\). In this paper we present the following new lower bounds:
\\(A_2(18,4) \ge 5632\\), \\(A_2(21,4) \ge 40960\\), \\(A_2(22,4) \ge 81920\\), \\(A_2(23,4)
\ge 163840\\), \\(A_2(24,4) \ge 327680\\), \\(A_2(24,10) \ge 136\\), and \\(A_2(25,6) \ge
17920\\). The new lower bounds are a result of a systematic computer search over
transitive permutation groups.
