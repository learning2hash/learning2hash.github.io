Consider the probability that a binomial random variable Bi\((n,m/n)\) with
integer expectation \(m\) is at most its expectation. Chv\'atal conjectured that
for any given \(n\), this probability is smallest when \(m\) is the integer closest
to \(2n/3\). We show that this holds when \(n\) is large.
