For nonnegative integers (n) and (d) let (A(nd)) be the maximum cardinality of a binary code of length (n) and minimum distance at least (d). We consider a slight sharpening of the semidefinite programming bound of Gijswijt Mittelmann and Schrijver and obtain that (A(188)leq 70).
