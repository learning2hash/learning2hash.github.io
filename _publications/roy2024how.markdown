We show that two independent elephant random walks on the integer lattice
\(\mathbb\{Z\}\) meet each other finitely often or infinitely often depends on
whether the memory parameter \(p\) is strictly larger than \(3/4\) or not.
Asymptotic results for the distance between them are also obtained.
